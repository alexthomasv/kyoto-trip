STEP 1🏯 8:35 AM – 9:15 AM · Ginkaku-ji
Ginkaku-ji — Silver Pavilion銀閣寺 · ¥500 entry · Opens 8:30 AM
Despite its name, Ginkaku-ji was never covered in silver — its beauty is in the restraint. Zen sand garden (Ginshadan), stunning moss gardens, and a hillside trail with views over Kyoto. Less crowded and more intimate than Kinkaku-ji. At 8:35 AM you'll practically have it to yourself.
📸 Don't miss: The perfect cone of sand (Kogetsudai) and the Ginshadan sea-of-silver-sand ripple garden. Walk the hillside loop for a panoramic view over the temple and city.

Kiyomizu-dera — The Stage Temple清水寺 · ¥400 entry · Closes 6pm
A massive wooden terrace jutting out over a hillside with panoramic views of Kyoto. The main hall was built without a single nail. In late March, cherry trees around the terrace bloom pink against the city skyline. After the temple, walk downhill through Ninen-zaka and Sannen-zaka — Kyoto's most photogenic stone-paved lanes lined with traditional shops.
